About Mixed Media Studio

Stake Skateboards is based in Pretoria, South Africa and we manufacture skateboards from bamboo and recycled materials.
We chose bamboo due to its' strength, flexibility, natural water-resistance and ecological sustainability.
Stake Skateboards also recover waste HPDE plastic, which we recycle to produce low-cost, enviromentally-friendly skateboards.
